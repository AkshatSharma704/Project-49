Interesting Facts about Lion

  1. Lions are the only cats that live in groups.
  2. Female lions are the main hunters.
  3. A lion's roar can be heard up to eight kilometres away.

Interesting Facts about Horse

  1. Horses can sleep both lying down and standing up.
  2. Domestic horses have a lifespan of around 25 years.
  3. Horses can run shortly after birth.
